Tutorial
Use the arrows keys (up, down, left and right) to move the block. The goal is to fit the block inside the hole to pass to the next level.
The 'X' and 'O' shaped tile are activators for some hidden tiles, or bridges. Each one of them can be activated if the block is on top of it, with the exception of the 'X' tile which can only be activated if the block is vertically on top of it.
Red tiles are fragile, which means they fall if the block is vertically on top of them. Be careful!
